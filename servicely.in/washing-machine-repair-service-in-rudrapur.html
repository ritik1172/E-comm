Washing machine service typically refers to the maintenance and upkeep of a washing machine to ensure that it is functioning correctly and efficiently. This can include tasks such as cleaning the drum and filters, checking and replacing any worn or faulty parts, adjusting the machine's settings, and running diagnostic tests to detect any potential problems. Regular washing machine service can help to prevent breakdowns and extend the lifespan of the machine. Some manufacturers may also offer washing machine service as part of a warranty or service contract, and there are often independent repair and maintenance companies that provide these services as well.

There are several types of problems that can occur with a washing machine, including:
1: Not starting or turning on. 2: Not filling with water. 3: Not draining water properly. 4: Making unusual noises during operation. 5: Vibrating or shaking excessively. 6: Displaying error codes on the control panel. 7: Leaking water. 8: Not spinning or agitating properly. 9: Overheating or not heating water properly. 10: Displaying an unpleasant odor.
